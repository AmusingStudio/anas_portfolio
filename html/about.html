About Me

CT Photographer

Based in Hartford, Connecticut.

I am traveling to Cities, National Parks, Random towns in between. To showcase not only the beauty that everyone knows and appreciates. But the beauty that lies within the ordinary, life is not only full of beauty, but it's also full of the ugly, the sad, and forgotten. To show you a mix of what you want to see and what spotlights our imperfections.
